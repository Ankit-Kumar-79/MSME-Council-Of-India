MSME Promotion Council India is established to promote the development and growth of micro, small, and medium enterprises (MSMEs) in India. The Council was established in 2008 with the objective of providing a platform for MSMEs to showcase their products and services, access finance, and connect with potential customers and partners.

The Council works closely with the government, industry associations, and other stakeholders to create an enabling environment for MSMEs to thrive. It provides a range of services to MSMEs, including capacity building, market development, technology upgradation, and policy advocacy.

The MSME Promotion Council India is a non-profit organization has a nationwide network of offices and partners, and it works with a wide range of stakeholders, including MSMEs, industry associations, financial institutions, and government agencies.
